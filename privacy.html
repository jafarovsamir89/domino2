Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-29. This Privacy Policy explains what data Domino Telefon ("the game", "we") collects, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Who we are

Domino Telefon is an online multiplayer domino game. This policy applies to the mobile app and related online services.

2. Data we collect

Account data. When you sign in with Google, we receive a unique account identifier and basic profile information (such as your name and, if available, profile picture) to create your in-game profile.

Profile data. Your display name, avatar, and profile links that you choose to set in the game.

Gameplay data. Match history, ratings, coins and in-game economy activity, and game results.

Communications. Text chat messages and in-room voice communication used to let players interact during games.

Safety & moderation data. Reports, blocks, and moderation actions you create or that involve your account.

Technical data. Device and connection information, app logs, and your IP address. Your approximate region and language are derived from your IP address to show the game in an appropriate language.

3. How we use data

  • To create and maintain your account and profile.
  • To run matches, matchmaking, ratings, and the in-game economy.
  • To enable chat and voice between players.
  • To keep the service safe: moderation, anti-abuse, anti-fraud, and rate limiting.
  • To detect and fix technical problems and improve the game.
  • To select the appropriate language based on approximate region.

4. Voice and microphone

In-room voice chat uses your microphone only while you are in a room and only after you grant microphone permission. Audio is streamed between players for real-time communication. You can deny or revoke microphone permission at any time in your device settings; the game remains playable without voice.

5. Chat moderation

To keep chat safe, messages are automatically filtered for offensive language and rate-limited to prevent spam. You can report and block other players using the in-game tools.

6. Sharing with third parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with service providers that help us operate the game, including:

  • Google Sign-In for authentication.
  • Cloud hosting (Google Cloud Platform) for servers and storage.
  • Push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging), if enabled on your device.
  • Advertising: if ads are shown, a third-party advertising provider may process limited device/advertising identifiers to deliver and measure ads. We will update this policy with the specific provider before ads are enabled.

7. Data retention

We keep account and gameplay data while your account is active. Moderation records may be kept longer where needed for safety and abuse prevention. Logs are kept for a limited period for security and troubleshooting.

8. Your rights and choices

You can request access to or deletion of your account data. Account deletion removes your profile and associated personal data, subject to limited retention required for safety, fraud prevention, or legal reasons. To request deletion, use the in-game tools or contact us at the address below.

9. Children

The game is not directed to children under the age required by your local law and the app's age rating. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children below that age.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

11. International transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located in other countries. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

13. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at support@simplesoft.az.
